Epsilon

Epsilon is the fifth planet orbiting in the habitable zone of star Zeta1 Reticuli in the Zeta Reticuli System. It possesses rings, similar to the planet Saturn. these rings are theorized to be the remains of a destroyed moon that was broken apart by the gravitational shift forced by asteroid Indus when it was captured by the gravity of Epsilon 50 million years ago. Its most notable features are its three large moons, including Epirus, which is home to a very aggressive carnivore designated Xeno Rex.

Hypnos

Hypnos is the fifth planet orbiting star Delta Pavonis in the Delta Pavonis system. Hypnos has a thin atmosphere mainly composed of nitrogen and ammonia. The planet has one moon which is rich with rare metals and minerals such as unobtanium and is being mined since 2306. Hypnos was discovered in 2040 by Copernicus Space Telescope.
